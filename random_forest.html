Random Forest

using cforest trained with caret and run with 10,000 trees.

species level.

Random forest is a classifier lke LDA. Below we first see overall accuracy of the classifier and a confusion matrix that shows good and bad classificaitions. Random forest can then identify predictors that are the most important. This is done by sampling out of bag, identifying what variables cause the greatest mean decrease in accuracy. https://bmcbioinformatics.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/1471-2105-9-307

We used the R package caret to perform 10 k-fold cross validation of the random forest classifer cforest with 1000 sampled trees.
